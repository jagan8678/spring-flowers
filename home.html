About Loyola Loyola Public School is managed and administered by the members of the Society of Jesus (Jesuits). Two names will go down in history as pioneers of LPS, Late Rt. Rev. Ignatius Mummadi, Bishop of Guntur, Catholic Diocese endeavored and brought the Jesuits too. “Born for greater things” – ‘Natus Ad Majora’ is the motto of LPS.

Principal's Message



Excellence is never an accident: LPS manifests its effort to honour, care for, and educate the whole person by encouraging its constituents to strive after intellectual, physical, psychological, social and spiritual health and well-being. Loyola seeks to encourage holistic growth, going beyond the academic context by providing members of its community with a wide variety of resources and opportunities...
